## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly
- **Account Information:** Name, email address, and password when you register.
- **Profile Information:** Travel preferences, home airport, frequent flyer numbers, and other optional profile details.
- **Booking & Trip Data:** Travel itineraries, destinations, dates, and trip notes you enter into the App.
- **Communications:** Messages or feedback you send to our support team.

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
- **Usage Data:** Features you use, pages you view, time spent in the App, and interaction data.
- **Log Data:** IP address, browser type, referring/exit pages, and timestamps.
- **Location Data:** With your permission, we may collect approximate or precise location to help with travel-related features (e.g., nearby airports, weather). You can disable location access in your device settings at any time.

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties
- **Authentication Providers:** If you sign in via Google, Apple, or another third-party service, we receive basic profile information per that service's privacy settings.
- **Travel APIs:** We may receive confirmation data from third-party booking or flight status services to populate your itineraries.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- **Provide and Operate the App:** Process trip data, manage your account, and deliver core app functionality.
- **Personalize Your Experience:** Tailor content, suggestions, and features to your travel history and preferences.
- **Send Notifications:** With your consent, send push notifications about trip reminders, flight alerts, or app updates.
- **Improve the App:** Analyze usage trends to enhance performance, fix bugs, and develop new features.
- **Communicate with You:** Respond to support requests, send important account notices, and provide customer service.
- **Ensure Security:** Detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, and security incidents.
- **Comply with Legal Obligations:** Meet applicable laws, regulations, and legal process requirements.

We do **not** sell your personal information to third parties.

## 8. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

- **Access:** Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
- **Correction:** Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
- **Deletion:** Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
- **Portability:** Request a machine-readable copy of your data.
- **Opt-Out:** Opt out of marketing communications at any time via in-app settings or by contacting us.
- **Withdraw Consent:** Where processing is based on consent, withdraw it at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address in Section 11.

### California Residents (CCPA/CPRA)
California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act. We do not sell personal information. For a full list of your CCPA rights or to submit a request, contact us directly.

### EU/UK Residents (GDPR)
If you are located in the European Economic Area or United Kingdom, our legal bases for processing your data include: performance of a contract, legitimate interests, compliance with legal obligations, and your consent. You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.
