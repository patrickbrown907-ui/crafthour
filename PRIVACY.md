# Privacy Policy for Craft Hour

**Effective date:** June 16, 2026

Craft Hour ("the app") is published by JT&M Software LLC ("we," "us"). This policy
explains how the app handles your information. The short version: **Craft Hour does
not collect, transmit, or share any of your personal data. Everything you enter
stays on your device.**

## Information we collect

**None.** Craft Hour has no account or login, and we do not collect, see, receive,
or store any of your information on our servers — we don't operate any servers for
this app.

Everything you create in the app — your projects, tracked time, expenses, prices,
and settings — is stored **locally on your device** in the app's private storage.
This data never leaves your device through the app, and we have no access to it.

## How your data is used

Your data is used only on your device, by you, to provide the app's features
(time tracking, pricing, and budgeting). It is not used for advertising,
analytics, profiling, or any other purpose.

## No tracking or third parties

Craft Hour contains **no analytics, no advertising, no trackers, and no third-party
data-collection SDKs**. The app does not make network requests with your data.

## Sharing and exports

The app can generate a budget summary as a PDF. This file is created on your device
and is only shared if **you** choose to share it (for example by email or AirDrop)
using your device's standard share sheet. We never receive a copy.

## Device backups

Because your data is stored on your device, it may be included in your own device
backups (such as iCloud or a computer backup) according to your iOS settings. Those
backups are controlled by you and Apple under Apple's terms — not by us.

## Deleting your data

You can delete individual projects, time entries, and expenses within the app at any
time. Deleting the app from your device permanently removes all data it stored.
Because we never receive your data, there is nothing for us to delete on our end.

## Children's privacy

Craft Hour is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect any
information from anyone — including children — because the app collects no data at all.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the version posted at this page and revise the
"Effective date" above.
